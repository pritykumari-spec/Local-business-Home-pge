About Us

Welcome to Sunrise, a cozy home bakery where every treat is made from scratch with love, care, and the finest ingredients. What started as a passion for baking in our home kitchen has grown into a heartfelt business dedicated to bringing joy through delicious, homemade sweets. From custom cakes and cupcakes to cookies and pastries, everything we make is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving something sweet, we're here to make life a little more delicious—one bite at a time

History

It all started with a little girl, a wooden spoon, and a big dream. Weekend mornings were filled with the smell of freshly baked cookies, flour-covered aprons, and lots of laughter in the kitchen. What began as a childhood love for baking slowly turned into a true passion—one birthday cake and cupcake batch at a time. After years of sharing treats with friends, family, and every neighborhood gathering, the dream of a home bakery felt too sweet to ignore. In 2010, Sunrise officially came to life right from our cozy kitchen. Today, we’re proud to bring a little homemade happiness to every order, baking each treat with the same love that started it all.

Ingredients & Sourcing

At Sunrise, we believe that delicious bakes start with the best ingredients. That’s why we use high-quality, locally sourced products whenever possible. From farm-fresh eggs and creamy butter to pure vanilla extract and seasonal fruits, everything we use is selected with care. We proudly support local farmers and small suppliers who share our commitment to freshness and sustainability. You won’t find any artificial preservatives or shortcuts in our kitchen—just honest ingredients and a whole lot of love in every batch.
